What's all this about?

You have lots of projects. Maybe you're working on the perfect cheesecake recipe, writing the great american novel, applying to grad school, or putting together a neighborhood blog. Some projects you finish, and some projects you don't. Some projects are longtime obsessions, and some are passing fancies. They swirl around, cluttering up your daydreams.

And that makes you awesome. You're interesting. People want to know more about you.

Do.Proje.cx is a tool for helping you share all the creative endeavors in your life by keeping an updated, central list of your projects. Do.Proje.cx is about fostering a shared excitement about personal creativity by providing a place where everybody can take a look at your latest creations, and you can see what everybody else has been doing, too.

How does it work?

Log in using your google account, and then list out your projects.

Leave notes for yourself and other on the status of your project.

Do.Proje.cx automatically links to posted URLs.

If you post a link to an RSS feed, Do.Proje.cx automatically fetches items from the feed and includes them as status messages in the project timeline.

Sharing your stuff

Each user can share their projects through their profile page. Each profile page also has a link to an RSS feed of recent project status updates.

Please note, do not add your project status RSS feed as a project status, or the internet will explode.
